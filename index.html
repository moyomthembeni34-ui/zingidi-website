About Us

Zingidi Investments and Projects is a South African-based supplier of office stationery and consumables. Established in 2024 and located in Edenvale, Gauteng, we proudly support business parks, small offices, and government institutions with reliable and cost-effective stationery solutions.

Our focus is on consistent supply, competitive pricing, and dependable service to ensure uninterrupted office operations.
